How to use this page during detention:

1. Use the Sort Options and Year Filter buttons to show the appropriate list of students.

2. Tick the checkbox next to each student who is physically present at detention and has successfully completed it.

3. If most students on the page attended, use Select All and then untick any students who are absent.

4. Click Successfully Completed Detention to update the selected students as having served detention.

5. If you mark a student by mistake, click Undo on that student's row to reverse the most recent served detention entry.

6. Use the Resolved column if you need to check whether a student is still pending or already marked as served.
